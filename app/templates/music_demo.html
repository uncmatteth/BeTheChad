How to Add to Your Pages

To add the music player to any page in your game, follow these steps:

Step 1: Add the container div

<div id="jukebox-container"></div>

Step 2: Include the JavaScript

<script src="/static/jukebox.js"></script> <script> document.addEventListener('DOMContentLoaded', function() { initJukebox('jukebox-container'); }); </script>

Alternative: Use an iframe

You can also embed the player using an iframe:

<iframe src="/music/player" width="320" height="120" frameborder="0"></iframe>
