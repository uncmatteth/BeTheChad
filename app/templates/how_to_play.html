How to Play Chad Battles

Getting Started

Welcome to Chad Battles, the ultimate game where your online persona becomes your character!

  1. Connect your Twitter account to create your chad
  2. We'll analyze your tweets to determine your Chad Class
  3. Customize your chad with items and equipment
  4. Battle other chads to earn Chadcoin and experience
  5. Collect waifus to boost your stats
  6. Join or create a cabal with other players

Chad Classes

Your tweets and online behavior determine your Chad Class, each with unique stat bonuses:

  • Alpha Chad: Natural leaders with charisma
  • Sigma Grindset: Lone wolves with focus
  • Gigachad: Legendary individuals with status
  • Meme Overlord: Masters of internet culture
  • Crypto Knight: Digital asset warriors
  • Ratio King: Social media tacticians
  • Normie Chad: Balanced individuals
  • KOL: Key Opinion Leaders who shape trends
  • Tech Bro: Startup enthusiasts who disrupt
  • Gym Rat: Fitness devotees
  • Debate Lord: Masters of rhetoric
  • Diamond Hands: Unshakeable investors
  • Lore Master: Knowledge keepers

Special Classes

Blockchain Detective: A rare, prestigious class reserved for proven on-chain investigators with a long history of detective work in the blockchain space.

Think you deserve this class? You can appeal your classification in your profile settings if you can demonstrate:

  • At least 1 year of active blockchain investigation activity
  • Evidence of significant on-chain analysis contributions
  • Recognition within the blockchain investigation community

Our system has anti-gaming measures to detect accounts created just to obtain specific classes or those engaging in suspicious activity patterns. These accounts may be assigned to special penalty classes.

Stats and Attributes

Your chad has four primary attributes that determine performance in battles:

  • Clout: Your overall influence and status
  • Roast Level: Your ability to attack other chads
  • Cringe Resistance: Your defense against attacks
  • Drip Factor: Your style and swag factor

Battles

Challenge other chads to battles to earn rewards:

  • Win battles to earn Chadcoin and experience
  • Level up to increase your base stats
  • Develop strategies based on your class strengths
  • Use items and waifus to gain an edge in battle

Waifus

Collect waifus to boost your stats and gain special abilities:

  • Waifus come in different rarities, from Common to Legendary
  • Each waifu provides unique stat bonuses
  • Equip waifus before battles for maximum advantage
  • Some waifus have special abilities that can turn the tide of battle

Cabals

Join forces with other players in cabals:

  • Create or join a cabal with like-minded chads
  • Participate in cabal wars for massive rewards
  • Contribute to your cabal's growth and prosperity
  • Share resources and strategies with cabal members

Economy

The game has a thriving economic system:

  • Earn Chadcoin through battles, quests, and activities
  • Spend Chadcoin on items, waifus, and upgrades
  • Trade with other players in the marketplace
  • Invest in cabal treasury for long-term benefits
