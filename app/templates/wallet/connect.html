How to Connect
  1. Select your preferred wallet provider.
  2. If you don't have the wallet installed, you'll be redirected to install it.
  3. Approve the connection request in your wallet.
  4. Once connected, you'll be able to mint NFTs and perform blockchain operations.
Why Connect a Wallet?

Connecting your wallet enables you to:

  • Mint your game assets as NFTs
  • Own your digital items on the blockchain
  • Trade your NFTs on marketplaces
  • Earn Chadcoin by burning NFTs
  • Participate in exclusive NFT events
