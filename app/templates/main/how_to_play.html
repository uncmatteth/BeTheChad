How to Play Chad Battles

Your guide to becoming the ultimate Chad in the meme-powered blockchain gaming universe!

Getting Started

1. Create Your Character

  1. Login with your X (Twitter) account
  2. Tweet MAKE ME A CHAD @RollMasterChad or use the in-app creation form
  3. Our AI analyzes your X profile to generate a unique Chad character
  4. Your Chad will have one of several classes, each with different stat distributions
  5. Receive your starter waifu and some Chadcoin to begin your journey

2. Understand Your Stats

Your Chad has four primary stats that determine battle outcomes:

  • Clout - Your overall power and influence
  • Roast Level - Your offensive capability
  • Cringe Resistance - Your defensive capability
  • Drip Factor - Your critical hit chance and status effect resistance

3. Character Classes

Class Specialty Best Stats
Crypto Chad Blockchain expertise, high wealth Clout, Drip Factor
Sigma Grinder High persistence, strong defense Cringe Resistance, Roast Level
Meme Lord Creative attacks, viral power Roast Level, Clout
Gym Bro Physical strength, endurance Cringe Resistance, Drip Factor
E-Chad Tech savvy, quick reflexes Drip Factor, Roast Level

4. Collect Waifus

Waifus are collectible companions that boost your Chad's stats:

  • Waifus come in different rarities: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
  • Each waifu provides unique stat bonuses
  • You can equip up to 3 waifus at once
  • Waifus can be won in battles, purchased in the marketplace, or earned through special events
  • Rare waifus provide stronger bonuses and unique special abilities
Game Mechanics

Battles

Challenge other Chads to battle for waifus and Chadcoin:

  1. Initiate a battle by tweeting CHALLENGE @opponent TO BATTLE @RollMasterChad or using the in-app battle system
  2. Your opponent must accept the challenge
  3. Both players can wager waifus or Chadcoin on the outcome
  4. Battles are turn-based with special moves based on your Chad class
  5. The winner gains XP, Chadcoin, and any wagered items
Battle Tips
  • Match your waifus to your character class for optimal bonuses
  • Use Meme Elixirs before important battles for temporary stat boosts
  • Different opponents have different weaknesses - adapt your strategy!

Cabals

Form a cabal with other players for group benefits:

  • Create or join a cabal of up to 10 Chads
  • All cabal members receive passive stat bonuses
  • Participate in cabal battles against other groups
  • Complete special cabal missions for exclusive rewards
  • Cabal levels increase as members win battles and complete missions
  • Higher level cabals provide better bonuses to all members

Leveling Up

Increase your Chad's power through these methods:

  • Win battles to gain XP and level up
  • Collect and equip better waifus
  • Acquire and use items to boost specific stats
  • Complete daily and weekly challenges for bonus XP
  • Participate in special events for rare rewards
NFT & Blockchain Features

Waifu NFTs

Turn your waifus into tradable NFTs on the Solana blockchain:

  1. Connect your Solana wallet to the game
  2. Mint your favorite waifus as NFTs for a small Chadcoin fee
  3. List your NFTs on the marketplace for other players to purchase
  4. Trade NFTs directly with other players
  5. Rare waifus have higher market value
NFT Benefits
  • Trade waifus with other players for profit
  • Mint limited edition waifus as valuable collectibles
  • Earn passive income through marketplace fees
  • Build your digital collection with proof of ownership

Chadcoin Economy

The in-game currency that powers the Chad Battles ecosystem:

  • Earn Chadcoin by winning battles
  • Spend Chadcoin on Meme Elixirs, items, and marketplace purchases
  • Pay Chadcoin fees for minting NFTs
  • Complete daily tasks for Chadcoin rewards
  • Hold Chadcoin to receive special airdrops and rewards
Marketplace Guide
  • Buy waifus and items from other players
  • Sell your minted NFTs for Chadcoin
  • Set your own prices for listed items
  • Monitor price trends to maximize profits
  • Look for undervalued waifus with good stat bonuses
Special Features

Meme Elixirs

Consumable items that provide temporary buffs to your Chad:

  • Sigma Serum - Boosts all stats by 10% for 6 hours
  • Degen Potion - +30% Clout, -10% Cringe Resistance for 12 hours
  • Wojak Tears - +25% Roast Level for 8 hours
  • Diamond Hands Essence - +40% Cringe Resistance for 6 hours
  • Pepe's Blessing - +35% Drip Factor for 8 hours
Pro Tip: Elixir effects don't stack! Using a new elixir will replace any active elixir effect.

Special Events

Limited-time activities with exclusive rewards:

  • Meme Wars - Large-scale battles between multiple cabals
  • Waifu Gacha - Special summons with increased rare waifu rates
  • Raid Boss Challenges - Cooperate with other players to defeat powerful opponents
  • Tournament Arcs - Competitive brackets with legendary rewards
  • Seasonal Challenges - Themed events with limited edition waifus and items

Community Features

Engage with other players:

  • Global and cabal chat systems
  • Trading marketplace for direct player-to-player exchanges
  • Leaderboards tracking top players and cabals
  • Community challenges with group rewards
  • Vote on future game features and updates
