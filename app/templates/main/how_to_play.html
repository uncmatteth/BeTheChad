How to Play Chad Battles

Your guide to becoming the ultimate Chad in the meme-powered blockchain gaming universe!

Getting Started

1. Create Your Character

  1. Login with your X (Twitter) account
  2. Tweet MAKE ME A CHAD @RollMasterChad or use the in-app creation form
  3. Our AI analyzes your X profile to generate a unique Chad character
  4. Your Chad will have one of several classes, each with different stat distributions
  5. Receive your starter waifu and some Chadcoin to begin your journey

2. Understand Your Stats

Your Chad has four primary stats that determine battle outcomes:

  • Clout - Your overall power and influence
  • Roast Level - Your offensive capability
  • Cringe Resistance - Your defensive capability
  • Drip Factor - Your critical hit chance and style factor

4. Collect Waifus

Waifus are collectible companions that boost your Chad's stats:

  • Each waifu provides unique stat bonuses
  • You can equip up to 3 waifus at once
  • Waifus can be won in battles, purchased in the marketplace, or earned through special events
  • Rare waifus provide stronger bonuses and unique special abilities

Game Mechanics

Battles

Challenge other Chads to battle for waifus and Chadcoin:

  1. Initiate a battle by tweeting CHALLENGE @opponent TO BATTLE @RollMasterChad or using the in-app battle system
  2. Prepare by equipping your best items and waifus
  3. The battle is turn-based, with each Chad taking actions
  4. The battle ends when one Chad's HP reaches zero
  5. Winners get more rewards and may claim opponent's waifus
  6. Even if you lose, you still gain some XP for participating
Battle Mechanics

Battle Phases:

  1. Preparation: Equip items and select your waifu companion
  2. Initiative: Highest Drip Factor moves first
  3. Attack Phase: Your Roast Level vs opponent's Cringe Resistance
  4. Special Abilities: Use class and waifu abilities tactically
  5. Resolution: First chad to reach zero HP loses

Damage Calculation: Base damage = (Attacker's Roast Level × Clout Modifier) - (Defender's Cringe Resistance × Defense Modifier)

Critical hits occur when your attack roll exceeds the opponent's defense by 50% or more, dealing 1.5× damage!

Battle Tips
  • Match your waifus to your character class for optimal bonuses
  • Use Meme Elixirs before important battles for temporary stat boosts
  • Different opponents have different weaknesses - adapt your strategy!
  • Consider your opponent's equipped waifus when planning attacks
  • Special moves are powerful but should be used strategically

Cabals

Form a cabal with other players for group benefits:

  • Create or join a cabal of up to 10 Chads
  • All cabal members receive passive stat bonuses
  • Participate in cabal battles against other groups
  • Complete special cabal missions for exclusive rewards
  • Cabal levels increase as members win battles and complete missions
  • Higher level cabals provide better bonuses to all members
Cabal Leadership Structure
  • Lord of the Shill: The cabal leader who grants +0.1 to all stats for all members
  • Clout Commander: Officer who grants 50% of their Clout stat to all members
  • Roast Master: Officer who grants 50% of their Roast Level stat to all members
  • Cringe Shield: Officer who grants 50% of their Cringe Resistance stat to all members
  • Drip Director: Officer who grants 50% of their Drip Factor stat to all members

Leveling Up

Increase your Chad's power through these methods:

  • Win battles to gain XP and level up
  • Collect and equip better waifus
  • Acquire and use items to boost specific stats
  • Complete daily and weekly challenges for bonus XP
  • Participate in special events for rare rewards

Turn your waifus into tradable NFTs on the Solana blockchain:

  1. Connect your Solana wallet to the game
  2. Mint your favorite waifus as NFTs for a small Chadcoin fee
  3. List your NFTs on the marketplace for other players to purchase
  4. Trade NFTs directly with other players
  5. Rare waifus have higher market value

  • Pay Chadcoin fees for minting NFTs
